# WebRTC Stats Extension

Welcome to the documentation for the WebRTC Stats Extension! 

## What is this tool?
Have you ever been on a video call (like Google Meet) or a voice chat (like Discord) and experienced robotic audio, frozen video, or heavy lag? Often, it's hard to tell if the issue is your internet, the other person's internet, or the platform itself.

This extension is like a **check-engine light for your internet calls**. 

It runs quietly in the background of your browser and monitors the technical vital signs of your connection (using a technology called WebRTC, which powers most modern voice and video apps). Instead of just showing you raw, confusing numbers, it translates this data into easy-to-understand visual indicators and plain-English insights.

## Who is this for?
- **Everyday Users**: To quickly see if their internet is causing their video call to lag.
- **Gamers & Streamers**: To monitor their connection latency (ping) in real-time on browser-based voice chats.
- **Developers & IT Support**: To debug and diagnose network issues without needing to open complex browser developer tools.
