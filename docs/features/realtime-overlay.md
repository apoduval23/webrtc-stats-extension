# Real-Time Overlay & Popup

## What is it?
When you are on a call, you don't want to leave the page or dig through menus to see if your connection is stable. This tool provides two seamless ways to view your call's health:

### 1. The Floating Overlay
As soon as you join a voice or video call, a sleek, semi-transparent widget automatically appears in the corner of your screen. 
- **Always Visible**: It floats above your meeting so you can keep an eye on your connection.
- **Draggable**: Is it blocking a button or someone's video feed? Simply click and drag the top bar to move it out of the way.
- **Non-Intrusive**: Clicking anywhere else on the widget passes your clicks straight through to the website behind it, so it never interrupts your workflow.

### 2. The Extension Popup
If you prefer a completely clean screen without any floating widgets, you can simply click the extension icon in your browser's toolbar at any time. A drop-down popup will instantly display the exact same real-time data as the overlay.

## Why is this useful?
It provides peace of mind. By having the data immediately accessible, you no longer have to guess if your connection dropped or if the platform is just being slow. You can see the data update every single second.
